========================================================
   HR Recruitment Website Form Privacy Policy Checkbox
========================================================

This module is an add-on to ``hr``.

HR Recruitment Website Form Privacy Policy Checkbox

This module adds a mandatory checkbox on the apply form for jobs for agreeing on the Privacy Policies.
